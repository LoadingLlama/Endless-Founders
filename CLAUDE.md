# Endless Founders - Project Context

## What This Is
Endless Founders (endlessfounder.live) is a 6-week summer startup house for 12-16 top student founders. Zero cost, zero equity. Flights, housing, food covered. Demo Day with VCs. Summer 2026 inaugural cohort. Run by Caden Chiang (UC Berkeley).

## Sponsor Outreach
The file `sponsor-tracker.md` in this repo tracks ALL sponsor outreach: who's been emailed, their status (replied, no reply, bounced, meeting booked, declined), and new leads to email.

### Email Template
**Subject:** Partnership Opportunity: Summer Startup House for Top Student Founders

**Structure:**
1. "Hello [Name]," (or "Hello," for general inboxes)
2. Intro: "I'm Caden, a student at UC Berkeley. I'm building a 6-week summer startup house..."
3. Traction stats:
   - 1,000+ waitlist signups in 3 days
   - 150+ completed applications from top universities
   - 200K+ LinkedIn impressions
   - Student ambassadors across 7 top universities
4. Partners: "Level Up Ventures, Stanford Venture Fund, Stanford Venture Fellowship, and SH1P" (Level Up first, then Stanford)
5. **Personalized paragraph** about why that specific company/person is a good fit
6. Sponsor benefits:
   - Direct access to 12-16 high-caliber founders (NOTE: website says 12-16, not 15-20)
   - A speaking opportunity during the program
   - Brand visibility across all channels and content
   - Presence at Demo Day
7. "Sponsorship tiers range from $3,000 to $50,000."
8. CTA: "Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call?"
9. Link: endlessfounder.live (render as clickable URL in HTML emails)
10. "Best, Caden"

### Style Rules
- Use HTML format (text/html) for Gmail drafts so the link renders clickable
- NO em dashes anywhere in emails
- Use "Hello" not "Dear" or "Hi"
- Keep personalized paragraph specific to the company (reference their programs, products, mission)
- Content posted daily, targeting ~100K impressions/day across channels

### Program Terms (for replies like DRF's "what are the terms?")
- Zero cost, zero equity, no strings attached
- Flights, housing, food all covered
- 12-16 founders per cohort
- 6-week residency, Summer 2026
- 1:1 mentorship, Demo Day, warm investor intros
- Structure optional, nothing forced
- Location TBA

### Key Responses
- If company offers discount instead of sponsorship: We reserve brand placement for official sponsors. Ask them to sponsor or offer product for free in exchange for visibility (daily content, ~100K impressions/day, Demo Day).
